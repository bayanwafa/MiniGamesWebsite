Welcome to the X and O Showdown against the Computer Challenge!

Prepare for an epic showdown in the classic game of X and O, also known as Tic-Tac-Toe. Challenge a friend to a strategic duel, taking turns to mark the grid with your X or O. Be the first to complete a row, column, or diagonal to claim victory! Ready to prove your tactical skills? Let's get started!

You have to check this before you start playing:

  1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  2. Two players are involved, one being the human player and the other being the computer.
  3. The human player uses "X," while the computer uses "O."
  4. Players take turns placing their symbol in an empty grid cell.
      5. The human player starts.
  5. The goal for both the human player and the computer is to be the first to form a row, column, or diagonal with three of their symbols.
      6. This can be done horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  6. The game ends when one of the following occurs:
      7. The human player or computer successfully creates a winning combination.
      All cells are filled, resulting in a draw.
